How to Play
    In the game of one there are two different conditions you need to meet to play a card, a card with the same number or a card with the same color.
    A card can be played on top of another if they share a number on the card, meaning any colored 7 can be pplayed ontop any other colored seven.
    A card can also be played on any other card that shares a color and suit with that card, meaning any red card can be played on another red card.
    What to do if none of your cards can be played? Draw a new card! cards can be drawn at any time by clicking on the center left card with the One logo on it.
    How do I actually play cards? cards can be played by clicking a dragging a card onto the playstack. (center right) this means you have to use the card you just played as a base to play your next card!
